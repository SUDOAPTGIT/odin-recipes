Lasagna

About

A classic Italian dish that layers rich flavors and comforting textures.

This traditional lasagna recipe features layers of tender pasta sheets, a savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and a blend of melted cheeses. The key to a perfect lasagna lies in the balance of flavors and the slow baking process that allows the ingredients to meld together beautifully. Fresh herbs like basil and oregano enhance the taste, while a touch of nutmeg in the béchamel adds depth.

Serve your homemade lasagna hot from the oven, garnished with fresh parsley or basil. This dish is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions, as it brings warmth and satisfaction to any meal.

approx time to make: 1 hour 30 minutes.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 4 cups milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked according to package instructions)
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes. Gradually whisk in milk, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Cook until thickened.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce. Add a layer of noodles, followed by béchamel sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and topped with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  7. Let lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil.
    8. Enjoy!
