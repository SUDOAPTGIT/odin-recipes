Pizza

About

A timeless Italian favorite that brings joy to tables everywhere.

This classic pizza features a hand-tossed dough base topped with a tangy tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and your choice of toppings. Whether you prefer the simplicity of a Margherita or the richness of a loaded meat pizza, the key to perfection lies in using quality ingredients and baking at high temperature until the crust is golden and crispy. The combination of fresh basil, olive oil, and perfectly melted cheese creates an unforgettable flavor profile.

Serve your homemade pizza hot from the oven and enjoy it with family and friends. The beauty of pizza is its versatility—customize it with your favorite toppings, experiment with different cheeses, and make it your own. Whether it's a casual weeknight dinner or a special gathering, pizza always brings people together.

approx time to make: 1 hour.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon sugar

For the Topping:

  • ¾ cup tomato sauce
  • 1½ cups fresh mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¼ cup fresh basil (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Optional Toppings:

  • ½ cup pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (diced)
  • ½ onion (diced)
  • ¾ cup Italian sausage (crumbled)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, then knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Place dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Punch down the dough and stretch it into a 12-inch round shape on a floured surface.
  6. Transfer dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet. Brush with olive oil.
  7. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a ½-inch border for the crust.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  9. Add your desired toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, sausage, etc.).
  10. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  11. Remove from oven and garnish with fresh basil.
  12. Let cool for 2-3 minutes, slice, and enjoy!
